About Us

The CrowdSourced Environmental Reporting platform offers an easy-to-use system for users to report problems related to garbage, sewage, and drainage. By visiting the "Complaints" section, users can fill out a form that specifies the issue and its location. To assist authorities in better assessing the problem, users can also attach photos or videos. Our goal is to ensure prompt action on user complaints and maintain a safe, healthy city environment.
